How to Book

1. Choose your Route

Select your starting station and destination from our available routes. Check the Fare Guide to see the ticket price for your trip!

2. Register & Book

Create an account and log in to reserve your seat. Choose your preferred schedule, then confirm your booking!

Ride & Enjoy

Present your ticket at the station and hop aboard! Sit back, relax, and enjoy the ride on the Chiikawa Express!
